COVID-19 Regualtions

17 August 2021 Dear Mooikloof Ridge Resident, We hope that you and your loved ones are safe and healthy. The following is a reminder of items to be highlighted in terms of Lockdown level 3:

• The clubhouse and other facilities are opened. Please note that the Clubhouse will accommodate 50% of its maximum capacity, a maximum of 30 people will be permitted. for functions.

• The curfew between 22:00 to 04:00 is applicableand 05:00, will no longer be applicable.

• The wearing of face masks when leaving your residence is mandatory. A person must wear a cloth face mask or a homemade item that covers the nose and mouth, or another appropriate item to cover the nose and mouth. The wearing of a face mask is mandatory for every person when in a public place, excluding a child under the age of six years.

• The existing Estate and Scheme rules pertaining to consumption of alcohol still applies, no consumption of alcohol in public areas.

• The Estate Office hours will be from 09:00 to 15:00 daily and will operate under strict COVID 19 protocol. Resident are urged to conduct matters electronically by emailing the office at office@mooikloofridge.com or reception@mooikloofridge.com or the Managing Agent daveg@pretor.co.za.

Other measures may be implemented as required and communicated as necessary.
